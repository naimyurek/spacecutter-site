Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 21, 2025  ·  Developer: Naim Yurek  ·  yureklabs@gmail.com

Thank you for playing Space Cutter ("the Game", "the App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our mobile application. By downloading and playing Space Cutter, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

Space Cutter does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number.

The following data may be collected automatically:

  • Advertising ID: A resettable device identifier used by Google AdMob to serve personalized or non-personalized ads.
  • Usage data: Crash reports and general analytics collected by the platform (Apple) to help us improve stability.
  • Game progress: Level number, coins, purchased items, and settings are stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences. This data never leaves your device.

We do not collect: name, email, location, health data, contacts, photos, or any other sensitive personal information.

2. Advertising

Space Cutter uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use certain information from your device to provide relevant ads. This may include:

  • Advertising ID (IDFA on iOS / GAID on Android)
  • IP address (for general location approximation)
  • Device information (model, OS version)

Google's use of advertising data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

You can opt out of personalized advertising at any time by going to Settings → Privacy → Apple Advertising on your iOS device and disabling "Personalized Ads".

3. Data Storage

All game progress (level, coins, unlocked items, settings) is stored locally on your device. We do not operate any servers that store your personal data. Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data.

4. Children's Privacy

Space Cutter is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at yureklabs@gmail.com and we will promptly delete it.

5. Third-Party Services

The app integrates the following third-party SDKs:

  • Google AdMob — Advertising. Privacy Policy
  • Flutter / Dart (Google) — App framework. No data collection beyond crash reporting opted in via device settings.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties, except as described above through AdMob for advertising purposes. We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our legal rights.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights including:

  • The right to know what data is collected about you
  • The right to request deletion of your data
  • The right to opt out of personalized advertising

To exercise these rights or for any privacy-related requests, contact us at yureklabs@gmail.com.

8. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information associated with the App. Since game progress is stored locally on your device, its security depends on your device's security settings.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact:
